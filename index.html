About Us

CENOZ Motors Company Limited was established in 2017, specialising in luxury vehicles and accessories. CENOZ Motors is now environmentally-focused and centers around the upper-class market. The company is managed by a team of knowledgeable individuals with over 15 years of combined experience in the Automobile Sales luxury vehicles. Our target markets are those who can simply afford it, who want to add another BMW to their collection, or ones who like to show off.
